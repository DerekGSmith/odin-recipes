Cheese Tortellini

Description

This recipe is for a Cheese Tortellini pasta with a marinara base. Serves 1 or 2 people and is a very quick cook.

Ingredients

  • 12-16 Ounces of Cheese Tortellini
  • 28 Ounces of Crushed Tomatoes
  • 2 Tablespoons of Olive Oil
  • 1 Tablespoon of Balsamic Vinegar
  • 2 Teaspoons of Garlic Powder
  • 1 Teaspoon of Dried Oregano
  • 1 Teaspoon of Salt
  • 3 Cups of Spinach Leaves
  • 8 Basil Leaves
  • 4 Ounces of Cream Cheese

Steps

  1. Prepare the tortellini according to package directions
  2. Place everything besides cream cheese into skillet and simmer for at least 10 minutes
  3. Add pasta and cream cheese into skillet
