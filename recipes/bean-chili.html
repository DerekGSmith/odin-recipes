Black Bean Chili

Description

This recipe is for a black bean chili. The recipe is vegetarian and very simple to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 Yellow Onion
  • 3 Garlic Cloves
  • 2 Tablespoons Olive Oil
  • 4 Tablespoons Butter
  • 1/3 Cup Quinoa
  • 45 Ounces of Black Beans
  • 56 Ounces of Canned Diced Tomatoes
  • 15 Ounces of Corn
  • 1 Tablespoon Mustard
  • 1/2 Cup Ketchup
  • 1 Tablespoon Worcestershire Sauce
  • 2 Tablespoons Chili Powder
  • 2 Tablespoons Oregano
  • 1 Tablespoon Garlic Powder
  • 1 Tablespoon Cumin
  • 1 Teaspoon Smoked Paprika
  • 3/2 Teaspoons Salt
